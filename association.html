dEURO Association
The purpose of the association is to promote the dEURO protocol. The association contributes to the further development of the system and informs the public through appropriate channels (website, events, social media, token listings, etc.). The association may develop and operate a front end to simplify access, but does not intervene in the system, does not actively trade in cryptocurrencies and does not offer any services to third parties. The association does not seek to make a profit and does not undertake any regulated activities.

Please note: The dEURO Association does not hold any DEPS and therefore has no influence on governance decisions.
